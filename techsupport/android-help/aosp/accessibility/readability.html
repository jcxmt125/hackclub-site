Android help - AOSP / accessibility / readability

Android allows you to adjust your text size, as well as some more options.
Head to settings, by opening the settings app, or swiping down twice from the top of the screen and tapping the cogwheel.
Scroll down to find "Accessibility", then tap it.
Scroll down to find "Display size and text", and tap it.
Use the sliders below to adjust the text size, or the display size.
Play around to find your preferred setting.
Use the bold text or High contrast text settings if required.
