Android help - AOSP / accessibility / colors

The "color and motion" setting on your device allows you to change multiple aspects of your phone.
This includes inverting your phone's colors, setting it to mainly be dark or bright, adjusting for colorblindness and more.
To access the menu, head to settings by opening the settings app, or swiping down twice from the top of the screen and tapping the cogwheel.
Scroll down to find "Accessibility", then tap it.
Scroll down to find "Color and motion", and tap it.

Color correction allows you to adjust your screen for colorblindness.
Color inversion will invert all colors on your device, including photos, apps, and more.
Dark theme will make your phone use darker primary colors.
Remove animations will reduce the amount of movement on your screen.
Large mouse pointer will enlarge your mouse pointer.
